# Amentum (amentum)

Amentum Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: AMTM) is a global advanced engineering and technology solutions company serving U.S. federal customers across defense, intelligence, energy, space, and civilian missions, with allied work in the United Kingdom and Australia. The company was created from the spin-off of AECOM's Management Services business in 2020 and on September 27, 2024 completed a Reverse Morris Trust transaction with Jacobs Solutions to merge Jacobs' Critical Mission Solutions (CMS) and Cyber & Intelligence (C&I) businesses into Amentum. Post-merger Amentum reports approximately $14.4 billion in FY2025 revenue, a $47.1 billion backlog, ~53,000 employees in 80 countries, and is headquartered in Chantilly, Virginia.

## Capability Areas

- **Mission Modernization & Sustainment** — lifecycle engineering, operations, training, logistics, and sustainment across all warfighting domains
- **Space Systems** — prime contractor for NASA's Exploration Ground Systems Program at Kennedy Space Center (Artemis)
- **Digital Transformation** — IT modernization, cloud, software engineering, data platforms, managed services
- **Sustainability & Environment** — environmental remediation, climate resilience, hazardous waste, munitions response
- **Advanced Energy Solutions** — nuclear, clean energy, and energy infrastructure including Owner's Engineer for the UK's first Small Modular Reactors
- **Data Analytics & Cyber** — mission cyber, intelligence analytics, and AI/ML inherited from Jacobs' Cyber & Intelligence portfolio

## Customers

U.S. Department of Defense (Army, Air Force, Navy & Marine Corps, SOCOM), the U.S. Intelligence Community, NASA, U.S. Department of Energy (LLNL, INL), U.S. Department of Homeland Security (USCIS biometrics at 130 locations), allied governments (UK MoD, Australia), and U.S. civilian customers (CAL FIRE).
