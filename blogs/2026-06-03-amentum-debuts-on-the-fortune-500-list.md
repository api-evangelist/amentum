Amentum has been ranked #313 on the Fortune 500 list, recognizing the company's position as one of the largest U.S. corporations by revenue. The milestone reflects the dedication of approximately 50,000 employees across more than 70 nations who deliver advanced engineering and technology solutions in national security, energy, space, and infrastructure sectors. Company leadership emphasized that the achievement demonstrates their commitment to innovation and problem-solving on a global scale.
